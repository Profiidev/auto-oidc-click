# Privacy Policy

**Auto Oidc Click** is built with your privacy in mind.

## No Data Collection

This extension does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information, browsing history, telemetry, analytics, or user data of any kind.

## Local Storage

All data associated with the extension, including your custom click rules, element selectors, and toggle settings, is saved entirely locally on your device using your browser's local storage. This information never leaves your machine and is never sent to our servers or any third parties. Only if you have synced your browser data with a cloud service (e.g., Chrome Sync, Firefox Sync) will your settings be stored in the cloud, but this is managed by your browser and not by us.

## Third-Party Access

Because we do not collect any data, we do not share or sell any data to third parties.

## Contact

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy, please review the open-source code or open an issue in the project repository.
